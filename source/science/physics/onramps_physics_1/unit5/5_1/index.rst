Learning Objectives
===================

1. Describe Uniform Circular Motion
2. Calculate rotational quantities (angular displacement, speed, and acceleration)
3. Use Rotational Kinematics to solve quantitative and qualitative problems
4. Define Newtonian Gravitation
5. Apply Newtonian Gravitation Principles to solve quantitative and qualitative problems

* Angular Displacement
* Angular Speed
* Angular Acceleration
* Newtonian Gravitation
